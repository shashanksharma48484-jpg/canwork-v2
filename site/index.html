📖 How to Use CanWork Visualizer

Each rectangle = one of 508 Canadian NOC 2021 occupations. Grouped by category. Hover for quick scores · Click for full detail panel · click StatCan link to open official page.

⭐ Opportunity Score

Weighted composite: Demand×0.30 + Income×0.25 + Transferability×0.20 + Newcomer×0.15 − AI Risk×0.10
Higher = better overall opportunity in Canada.

🌍 Quick Preset Filters

  • Best for Newcomers — Newcomer ≥ 7, Demand ≥ 7
  • Low AI Risk — AI Risk ≤ 3
  • High Income — Income ≥ 8
  • In Demand — Demand ≥ 8
  • Transferable + Safe — Transferability ≥ 7, AI Risk ≤ 5
  • Top Opportunity — Opportunity Score ≥ 6
